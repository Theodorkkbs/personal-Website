Intro

Hello my name is Theodore

From a young age i had a programming and technological affinity, this passion led me to enroll to Polytechnic University of the Aegean in the department of Product and System design engineering. My stream of study, in this 10 semesters university, was mostly around interactive systems and engineering.

University

During my university years i had the chance to learn about electrical engineering and get my hands on electronics like arduino or raspberry, create websites with front-end and backend, practise low level programming in microcontrollers and use tools like oscilloscope or multimeter.

Due to the designing affinity of my university, i was lucky to learn alot about design methodologies, UI/UX and apply them succesffuly on my projects.

Internships

Additionally, when i succesffully completed all the courses offered by my university, leaving Thesis and internship as the last two, i decided to pause my studies and fulfil my compulsory army services. Luckily, after 3 hard months on greek-turkish borders, i joined the IT department in the Ministry of National Defense. My role there was mostly debugging and testing a newly created web application called efedros.army.gr, other than that i used python to explore large datasets, visualize them and pass them to my supervisors. This internship lasted 6 months and i got a letter of recommendation from Colonel Ltn. Mr.Petrinos.

My second internship, took place in a company called Hellas Digital, an amazing electronics company with a higlhy educated CEO who was close to our small team at all times. My role there was to develop prototypes that would be a part of a bigger project. The prototypes were microcontrollers like Arduino or Esp32 and i had to establish connection between devices, connect these devices with IoT platforms like Thingsboard or Thingspeak and retrieve data through sensors. This internship lasted 3 months due to the fact that i couldnt be present due to covid-19 and impossible to have my hands on so many electronics from home.

My last internship, is taking place right now in ONEX SA, a Greek-American company that is a Security provider and also owns 2 out of the 3 shipyards in Greece. My current role there is to developer web applications for their CRM and HRM. My tasks so far were to create a reporting system for the company's CRM, a pdf converter, an Excel Importer with a table to read the Excel Fields and right now is the creation of further modules for our HRM. I am currently working here since November 2020.
