Intro

Hello my name is Theodore

From a young age I had a programming and technological inclination, which led me to enroll to the Polytechnic University of the Aegean specifically in the Department of Product and System Design Engineering. During the ten-semester study in the university, the courses and my projects were mostly affiliated with interactive systems and engineering.

University

During my course of study, I had the chance to learn about Electrical Engineering and get my hands on electronics like Arduino or Raspberry. Also, I created websites with front-end and back-end, practise low level programming in microcontrollers and use tools like oscilloscope or multimeter.

Due to the designing nature of my university, I was fortunate enough to learn a lot about design methodologies, UI/UX and apply them successfully on my personal projects.

Internships

When I successfully completed all the courses offered by the university, final Thesis and internship as the remaining final two to graduate, I decided to pause my studies and complete my mandatory military service. Luckily, after three hard months of training and serving on the Greek - Turkish borders, I joined the IT Department in the Ministry of Defense. My position there was mostly debugging and testing a newly created web application called efedros.army.gr. Apart from that, I used Python to explore large datasets, visualize and pass them to my supervisors. This internship lasted six months and as a result, it offered me a letter of recommendation from Colonel Mr. Petrinos

My second internship took place at company called Hellas Digital, an amazing electronics company with a highly educated CEO, who was available and close to our small team at all times. There, my main role was to develop prototypes, which would be a part of a bigger project. The prototypes were microcontrollers like Arduino or Esp32 and my task was to establish connection between devices, connect these devices with IoT platforms like Thingsboard or Thingspeak and retrieve data through sensors. This internship lasted for three months, due to the unfortunate fact, that I couldn’t be present due to Covid-19 regulations and since I was working from home, it was impossible for me to have access to a plethora of electronics.

My current internship, is taking place in ONEX S.A., a Greek-American company, which is a security provider and also owns two out of the three shipyards in Greece. My position there, involves me to develop web applications for the company’s CRM and HRM. My tasks so far, were to create a reporting system for the company's CRM, a PDF converter, an Excel Importer with a table to read the Excel Fields and right now, is the creation of further modules for our HRM. I am currently working here since November 2020.
